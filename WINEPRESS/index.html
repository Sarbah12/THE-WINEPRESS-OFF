The Heart Behind This

What is The Winepress?

The winepress is an ancient image — grapes crushed so that something new can flow. It is the place of pressure, of process, of transformation. This site was born out of that very season.

Here you will find devotionals rooted in Scripture, real testimonies from people who walked through fire, and resources to anchor you in the Word when life feels unsteady.
